15th August. Independence Day. Means nothing to me now. Not in school anymore. Honestly, it’s more like independence for me, I always hated going there just to watch everyone do their own little flag hoisting. Why not have one flag for the whole town and be done with it?

Today’s “independence” was from online payments. Spent 6–7 hours fighting with Airtel to disconnect my Airfiber. Easy to install, hell to uninstall. Calls, emails, customer care loops. Got it done through email. Billing stopped. Not paying a single rupee. They said prepaid, acted like postpaid. Scam. Not giving them a penny.

New connection coming. Offline payment. No more begging others to pay for me. Grateful for that.

Did some running. No gym. Morning dad was happy, night he was down because of dental pain. Showed him videos from top dentists in India. He chilled out after that. He just trusts the wrong people sometimes, and wrong info can wreck your life. Always trust the right professionals. Not random broke people with opinions.

People here have crab mentality. Tell you you can’t fix something. Plant negativity in your head. But when you compare their words to what the pros say, facts, logic, real examples, you know who to believe.

Day wasn’t productive. Didn’t track my stuff. But one big problem is gone. Dad’s mood is better. That’s enough for today.

- I am greatful for disconnection

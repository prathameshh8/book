Today was 5-9-2025. Eid.

Dad had a terrible day. Woke up saying he can’t take this shit anymore. It’s not even the tooth pain, it’s the mental torture of it. The caps he got fitted don’t feel like real teeth and it’s driving him insane. We just sit there helpless, watching.

He planned to go to Nagpur to fix it, but the plan got cancelled because the guy with the car dropped out. And we don’t have a car. That’s the problem. If you need to travel long distance, you need your own car. Otherwise you’re stuck hopping buses and rickshaws, feeling helpless.

A car is a big deal. A luxury for me right now. But I’ve set my standards. I can’t live without one. I’ll work and get it, even if it’s just an Alto. It will help my dad. It will help my mom. And it will change life.

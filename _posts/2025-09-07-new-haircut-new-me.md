Today was Sunday and I woke up late around 11, completely exhausted from yesterday’s travel. The first thing I did was go to the barber and get a new haircut. I am happy with it because it feels like a new me. I have this theory that if your hair is long, then your head is like a vessel that is already full and cannot take in more knowledge. But when you cut your hair short, the vessel becomes empty again and ready to absorb new knowledge.

The haircut is almost a buzz cut, not exactly but close. I told my barber how I wanted it and he gave me that look. I don’t look great with it, in fact I look kind of shit, but still it changes something in my character. I feel sharper and more in control. My jawline is visible but there is still a little fat on my face. If I lose that fat then I know I can look the best version of myself because a buzz cut with a sharp jawline always looks good.

The rest of the day was not very productive, but at least I started it with some action. The haircut gave me a little bit of momentum and I think that counts.

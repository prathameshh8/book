Today felt like one of the best days.

I started using my own web app properly and instantly saw so many impractical things. That alone is going to make the product sharper and more valuable.

Mom got sick today. She was on fast. Dad had a blood test.

Still, everything feels like it’s moving in the right direction. Like things are pivoting toward more happiness.

I did stress about funding for a bit. But honestly, at 17 or 18 you don’t need a funded startup. Just build something independent, get financial freedom, buy some cars, enjoy youth. After 25 maybe build a “real” SaaS, apply to YC, go bigger.

For now it’s simple: my parents are getting older, they’ve never seen the world, and that’s priority one. Get independence, travel with them, good dinners, marriage. Then think YC.

Overthinking never teaches you anything. Doing does. Who knows if this app will even need funding. You figure that out by acting, not sitting.

I don’t care about billions right now. 5–10 million is enough to live luxuriously off interest alone. That’s doable independently. The only focus is ticking off my goal list. Nothing else.

- I am greatful for going in right direction
- I am greatful for the indi community

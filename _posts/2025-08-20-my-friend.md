Got some sunlight, went for a run, and reconnected with a friend I hadn’t talked to in a long time. Found out another friend has typhoid and is in the hospital. Also planned with my friend when we’ll go out, eat pizzas, just chill.

Washed my bean bag so I can sit in the sun like the main character of a movie. Because I am the main character of my movie.

Realized I’ve been less selfish lately. Need to bring that back. Selfishness is power. I earned 16,000 rupees at 16, online. Who the fuck does that? That’s value. I just forget it sometimes.

I’m at the wrong time and wrong situation, but that’s on me. I’m responsible. For waking up early. For what I watch. For taking action instead of watching endless courses. It’s all on me.

We also got a notice about some financial security issue linked to my dad. Nothing huge, could be cleared by selling 3–4 gold rings. Hopefully the guy’s uncle pays since he got some government funding. Whatever. Small lesson: stay out of unwanted trouble, even if it’s relatives. Main characters don’t get dragged into other people’s mess.

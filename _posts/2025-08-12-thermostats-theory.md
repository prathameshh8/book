learned about thermostat theory
idea is you’ll do whatever it takes to maintain your standard
you’re not who you say you are, you’re who you accept to be
if you’re productive, healthy, meditating, running
you’ll fight to keep that momentum
that’s what momentum is to me

my version:
make a graph of your highs and lows
highs are productive days, healthy eating, no addictions
lows are lazy days, binge watching, skipping workouts
set your highs as the standard
when you drop even slightly, fight to get back up

same for money
if your standard is 8k/month, you’ll do anything not to go below it
hard to jump from 8k to 80k, but easy to keep 8k
thermostat theory explains that

Today I talked to my friend after about a week. I had been messaging around, asking others if they had seen him. Honestly, I don’t have many friends, and even though I used to dislike him before, I now enjoy hanging out with him. Maybe it’s not really about him but more about me being stuck inside the house all the time. When he comes over, we ride his scooter, and that becomes my only way to go out and feel social.

He never opened Telegram, maybe he even blocked me. I had been sending him some life lessons there, so who knows. Anyway, when we met, he told me his farming wasn’t going well. He had soybeans in his fields, but a virus hit the crops and now the plants are turning yellow.

That was the main update from him. Overall, I realized it’s okay not to depend too much on friends. It’s better if I just focus on my own things and keep moving forward.

Today we went to Amravati. Dad came back home suddenly from work, frustrated, saying he couldn’t take the pain anymore. So we decided to go see a dentist there.

The trip itself was rough. The bus was unhygienic, the rain made everything worse, and I was completely soaked. Civic sense is rare here, and traveling by bus reminded me why I dislike it so much.

But the dentist visit made it worth it. My dad got his bite properly aligned. The doctor was an orthodontist, well educated and professional. He mentioned that not every dentist gets alignment right, but he fixed it properly. After all the money already spent on treatment, at least this step was successful.

The day wasn’t pleasant overall, but the important part is that my dad’s teeth are finally aligned and he feels better now.

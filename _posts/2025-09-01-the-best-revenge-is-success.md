title: "The best revenge is success."
date: 2025-09-01
---

Today was that family event I used to dream about. I always pictured pulling up in a car, laptop in the backseat, iPhone in hand. But the date came and I didn’t even have a mobile of my own.

We arrived earlier than everyone else since we’re closer family, and I was just sitting there with nothing. That’s when it hit me—if you don’t take action, dates come and go, years pass, and nothing changes. Dreams stay dreams. Beliefs stay beliefs. Action is the only thing that makes them real.

Also, my hair is too long now. Tried growing it out but it just makes me look dull—like some tribal guy. Chubby cheeks too since I haven’t worked out in 2 weeks.

One more thing I noticed most of the teens my age there were talking nonsense, just people and events. None of them had the capability I have.

The best revenge is success. Nice cars, the lifestyle, and pulling up for real.
